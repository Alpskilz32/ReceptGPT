About Us

ReceptGPT is your ultimate cooking companion powered by AI. Our mission is to simplify the cooking experience by providing personalized recipe recommendations and helpful cooking instructions. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, ReceptGPT has something for everyone.

Our team consists of passionate developers and designers dedicated to delivering high-quality content and innovative features to enhance your culinary journey. We strive to make cooking fun, easy, and accessible to everyone.

Our Goals

We have set some ambitious goals to achieve:

  • Provide personalized recipe recommendations

  • Offer helpful cooking instructions for all skill levels

  • Make cooking fun, easy, and accessible to everyone

  • Promote social cohesion through shared cooking experiences
